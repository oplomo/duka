Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most of our items. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused and in the same condition as you received it. Please visit our returns page for more details.

2. How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can track your order directly on our website using the tracking number provided.

3. How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, it may be processed and shipped. To cancel your order, please contact our customer service team immediately.

4. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on your location.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by visiting the "Contact Us" page or emailing us directly at support@ourstore.com. We are available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.

6. Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

If your order has not been shipped yet, we can change the shipping address. Please contact customer service as soon as possible to request this change.

7. How do I apply a promo code to my order?

Promo codes can be entered at checkout. Simply apply the code in the "Promo Code" box, and the discount will be applied to your order total.

8. What should I do if my item is damaged during shipping?

If your item arrives damaged, please contact customer service immediately with photos of the damage, and we will assist you with a return or replacement.

9. Can I change the payment method after ordering?

Once an order has been placed, the payment method cannot be changed. If you need assistance, please contact us before completing your payment.

10. How long will it take to receive my refund?

Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to process. After your refund has been issued, you will receive a notification by email.

11. What types of payment do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

12. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards that can be redeemed on our website. You can purchase them directly from the "Gift Cards" section on our site.

13. Can I exchange an item?

Exchanges are only available on damaged or defective items. If you'd like to request an exchange, please contact our support team for assistance.

14. How can I change my account settings?

To change your account settings, simply log in and go to your profile page. You can update your personal information, change your password, and more.

15. Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please reach out to our sales team at sales@ourstore.com to inquire about wholesale opportunities.

16. What is your policy on customer reviews?

We encourage customers to leave reviews for products they’ve purchased. All reviews are moderated to ensure they meet our community guidelines.

17. How can I find my order history?

Once logged in, you can view your order history by visiting the "My Orders" section in your account settings.

18. What happens if I miss the delivery of my order?

If you miss the delivery, the courier service will attempt redelivery or hold your package at a nearby facility for pickup. You will be notified with further instructions.

19. How do I redeem my loyalty points?

Loyalty points can be redeemed at checkout for discounts. You can check your available points in the "My Account" section.

20. Can I subscribe to receive newsletters?

Yes, you can subscribe to our newsletter by entering your email address in the "Subscribe" section at the bottom of our homepage.
